Tips for bringing home a newly adopted pet

  • Create a designated area where your new pet can feel secure. Include essentials like a bed, food, water, and toys.
  • Remove hazards such as toxic plants, electrical cords, and small objects.Ensure that trash cans and cleaning supplies are out of reach
  • Set regular feeding, walking, and playtime schedules to help your pet adjust to their new environment.
  • Keep the atmosphere quiet and calm to avoid overwhelming your new pet.Avoid hosting gatherings or introducing too many new people initially.
  • Ensure your pet has a collar with an ID tag containing your contact information. Consider microchipping for added security.
